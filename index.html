About Us

Welcome to PG PLAS INC, a premier plastic items manufacturing company established in 2018. Located in Brampton, Ontario, Canada, we pride ourselves on delivering high-quality, innovative plastic solutions to meet the diverse needs of our clients.

At PG PLAS INC, we specialize in the production of a wide range of plastic products, ensuring durability, functionality, and sustainability. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility is equipped with the latest technology and operated by a team of skilled professionals dedicated to maintaining the highest standards of quality and efficiency.
